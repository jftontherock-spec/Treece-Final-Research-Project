# Treece-Final-Research-Project
# Problem: A popular fun-fact in Astronomy is that the moon is slowly drifting away from the Earth. This phenomenon, caused by water on Earth's surface, known as "Tidal Acceleration," has a less well known side effect on the Earth's rotation -- actually slowing it down. A thousand years ago, the moon was much closer to Earth and a day on Earth lasted a bit less than it does now! This project will a graph of the speed of the earth as a function of time.
# Method: Using RK4 in 6-dimentions, the differential equasions found by Sung-Ho Na and Seok Lee were solved.
# Results: The earth was found to be speeding up. Results could not be verified. 
# References: Na, Sung-Ho and Seok Lee. "Re-estimation of Earth's spin rotaional energy decrease due to tidal friction." Geoscience Journal, Vol. 18 No 1. p1-6, Association of Korean Geoscience Societies and Springer, March 2014.
